The Heroes

This adventure was planned, shot and told by Jordi and Robert, just a couple of regular dirtbags.

"Captain" Jordi

Adventure junkie and geek. Kayaking, surfing, trekking, diving... any fun activity close to nature makes him happy. This was his second kayak tour around Menorca Island and probably not the last. Jordi regulary writes about his outdoor experiences in his blog.

"Shipboy" Robert

Roberto has a great passion for outdoor activities. You can usually find him biking or hiking the Catalan territory at any given day. Great paddling buddy, this was his first serious experience with the kayak.
